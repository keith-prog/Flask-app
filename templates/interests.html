Fine Dining

My interests outside of my course and computers are many. Most of which I cannot afford to indulge in. A huge interest I have is fine dinning. When I was a chef I would usually have one night off and I try to find the best restaurant in the area and have a three to four course meal. When I lived in New York City there were plenty of high quality resauratnts to choose from. Some of my favorite New York restaurants are Jean-Georges , Daniel and Le Bernardin. The last time I was working and could afford to go out to a good restaurant once a week I was on the island of Saba. Saba is a very small island in the Caribbean with a population under two thousand. So as you could imagine there weren't to many restaurants on the island let alone great restaurants. The restaurants I liked in Saba were The Brigadoon and Chez Bubba Bistro . Unfortunately at the moment I cannot afford to partake in the fine dinning experience unless I cook for myself and that just defeats the purpose.
